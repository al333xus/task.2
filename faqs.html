Frequently asked questions

The original FAQ content has been reorganized into accordion sections so users can quickly open the question they need without scanning one long page of text.

What is the best way to rid my dog or cat of fleas?
Sometimes our furry friends pick up hitchhikers that make them itchy and uncomfortable. To eliminate fleas, choose a shampoo specially formulated to kill fleas and read and follow all instructions on the label. Repeat treatment as necessary until your pet is free of fleas. If skin irritation appears, stop use immediately and contact your local veterinarian.
How long can I expect my pet bird to live?
The answer varies depending on the type of bird, the size, the environment, and care quality. Cockatiels and budgies may live up to 20 years on average, while many parrots can live much longer. Longevity can often be supported by veterinary visits, healthy diet, and a sanitary environment.
How often should I feed my pet snake?
Most snakes eat one to two times per week depending on age, size, and activity level. Some snakes can go as long as two weeks between meals. If your snake has not eaten in two or three weeks and seems lethargic, contact your local veterinarian.
