Frequently asked questions

Quick answers from our Pexperts

These short answers help users get oriented quickly and move into deeper pet-specific content or consultations when needed.

What is the best way to rid my dog or cat of fleas?

Choose a shampoo specially formulated to kill fleas and carefully read and follow all label instructions. Repeat treatment as necessary until your pet is flea free. If you see skin irritation, stop use immediately and contact your local veterinarian.

How long can I expect my pet bird to live?

The answer can vary widely depending on the type of bird, size, environment, and overall care. Cockatiels and budgies may live up to around 20 years on average, while some parrot varieties can live nearly 100 years.

How often should I feed my pet snake?

Most snakes eat one to two times per week, depending on age, size, and activity level. If your snake has not eaten in two or three weeks and seems lethargic, contact your veterinarian.
