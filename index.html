What is the best way to rid my dog or cat of fleas?
Use a shampoo specially formulated to kill fleas and read the label carefully. Repeat treatment as needed until your pet is flea-free, and contact your veterinarian if skin irritation occurs.
How long can I expect my pet bird to live?
Lifespan varies by species, size, environment, and care quality. Cockatiels and budgies may live up to 20 years on average, while some parrots can live much longer.
How often should I feed my pet snake?
Most snakes eat once or twice per week, depending on age, size, and activity level. If a snake has not eaten in two or three weeks and seems lethargic, contact a veterinarian.
